Straight Drop outdoor blinds provide a clean, minimal finish—ideal for areas without posts or where side retention isn’t needed. Unlike track-guided or wire-guided systems, they don’t use side channels or wires, making them simpler to install and well-suited to narrow spaces, patios, or balconies where other systems don’t fit neatly.

TSBC’s Straight Drop blinds span up to 6 metres wide with a 3 metre drop and secure with a simple base anchor clip.

Smart ways to control yoru shade

TSBC Straight Drop blinds offer a range of control options—manual or motorised—to suit different spaces, budgets, and convenience needs.

Crank handle operation: Easy to use and requires no electricity. Ideal for areas without power access or where precise manual control is preferred.
Motorised with remote control, wall switch or App control: Adds convenience with one-touch operation. Great for multiple blinds or hard-to-reach installations.
Smart home integration: Compatible motor systems can sync with home automation, allowing timers, sensors, or voice control for better energy efficiency and comfort.
